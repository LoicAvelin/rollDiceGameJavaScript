Welcome to this two-player dice game. 👍🏻

The rules are so simple :

  1. 🎲 Roll the die as many times as you like, but you skip your turn if you roll a 1.
  2. 👇🏻 You pass your turn when you hold your score and your turn counter is reset to 0.
  3. 🏆 The first player to reach 100 points wins !
